## Sprint rule

> **1 Sprint = 1 clear product objective.**

**Sprint 69 objective:** Shadow becomes a **Browser Companion** — first true Shadow Client outside Lumen web/desktop shells.

**Principle:** *Learn where you are.*

**Not in this sprint:** IDE extension, Mobile app, Ambient voice, aggressive scraping, private repo analysis without consent.

---

# Vision

Shadow accompanies the user directly in the browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave; Firefox-ready architecture).

Content stays where it is. Shadow brings intelligence, memory, and continuity.
