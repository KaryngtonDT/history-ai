# Vision

## Mission

History AI is an AI-powered learning platform that transforms long-form educational content into personalized, multilingual learning experiences.

The platform allows users to consume complex knowledge through translated podcasts, intelligent summaries, timelines, interactive maps, flashcards, quizzes and AI conversations.

Instead of watching a two-hour lecture, users receive a complete learning experience adapted to their available time, preferred language and level of knowledge.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of high-quality educational resources are published every day.

Examples include:

* YouTube lectures
* University conferences
* Podcasts
* Interviews
* Historical documentaries
* AI research presentations

Most of this content is available only in English.

Many learners are unable to fully benefit from this knowledge because of:

* language barriers
* excessive video length
* lack of structure
* absence of revision tools
* inability to learn while commuting

Current translation tools only translate.

They do not transform information into knowledge.

History AI fills this gap.

---

# Vision Statement

History AI becomes the personal AI historian, teacher and researcher of every learner.

Instead of replacing the original creator, History AI augments the learning experience by making knowledge more accessible.

The application transforms passive video watching into active learning.

# Core Philosophy

Learning should adapt to humans.

Humans should not adapt to learning.

The platform automatically converts educational content into the best possible learning format.

# Non Goals

History AI is NOT:

* a YouTube replacement
* a social network
* a video hosting platform
* a piracy platform

History AI enhances learning while respecting copyright and applicable laws.
