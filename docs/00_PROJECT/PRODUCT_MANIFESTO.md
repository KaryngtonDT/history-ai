# PRODUCT MANIFESTO

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

---

# Our Mission

History AI exists to transform passive information into durable knowledge.

Reading is not learning.

Watching is not learning.

Listening is not learning.

Learning happens when information is transformed into understanding, memory, and action.

History AI exists to automate this transformation.

---

# The Problem

The world has more knowledge than ever.

Books.

Videos.

Podcasts.

Courses.

Articles.

Research papers.

AI has made information abundant.

Human attention has not increased.

People consume information every day.

Very little becomes long-term knowledge.

The problem is no longer access to knowledge.

The problem is knowledge retention.

---

# Our Vision

Every piece of knowledge should become learnable.

Every document.

Every lecture.

Every podcast.

Every conversation.

Every book.

History AI transforms raw information into structured learning.

---

# What We Build

History AI is not a summarization tool.

History AI is not a transcription tool.

History AI is not a translation tool.

Those are implementation details.

History AI is a Knowledge Transformation Platform.

Its purpose is to transform any knowledge source into a personalized learning experience.

---

# Core Principles

## Knowledge before Technology

Technology changes.

Knowledge does not.

The domain model must outlive every AI provider.

---

## AI is Infrastructure

LLMs are tools.

Speech recognition is a tool.

Translation models are tools.

None of them define the product.

---

## Source Independence

Knowledge can originate from anywhere.

PDF

Audio

Video

Books

YouTube

Websites

Research papers

Future sources must integrate without redesigning the platform.

---

## Learning First

Every feature must improve learning.

If a feature does not improve learning, it does not belong in the product.

---

## Human in Control

Users always own their knowledge.

AI assists.

AI never replaces human understanding.

---

# Product Philosophy

The objective is not to answer questions.

The objective is to create understanding.

Understanding creates memory.

Memory creates expertise.

Expertise changes lives.

---

# Long-Term Vision

History AI becomes a Knowledge Operating System.

History is only the first domain.

Future domains include:

Programming

Medicine

Law

Finance

Engineering

Languages

Science

Business

The platform remains identical.

Only the knowledge changes.

Understanding

Retention

Accuracy

Transparency

Extensibility

Quality

Not for hype.

---

# Engineering Principles

Business rules belong to the domain.

Infrastructure is replaceable.

Every external dependency is an adapter.

Architecture exists to protect the business.

---

# Definition of Success

Success is not measured by:

Number of AI models.

Number of supported providers.

Number of integrations.

Success is measured by one question:

"Did the user actually learn something?"

If the answer is yes,

History AI succeeded.

---

# Final Statement

History AI is not an AI application.

History AI is a platform that transforms information into knowledge.

Every architectural decision, every feature, every RFC, and every line of code must reinforce this mission.
