# Definition

**Shadow Continuity** is intellectual continuity across time, surfaces, and content types.

It is not "remembering a chat."

It is connecting:

- what you learned this morning
- what you apply this afternoon
- what you read this evening
- what you struggle with again three weeks later

Phase III adds **Presence** so continuity is not broken when the user leaves the browser.

---

# Principles

1. Continuity is **explainable** — Shadow cites sources and dates.
2. Continuity is **user-controlled** — disable proactive links anytime.
3. Continuity never **invents** connections — deterministic merge and graph rules only.
4. Continuity respects **privacy** — no background surveillance of apps or files.
